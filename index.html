Welcome to JobWallet Website

JobWallet, the only telco application that will help you find daily wage workers and daily wage jobs.

USER FRIENDLINESS

Data transition between user and the application is done through SMS and USSD menus that the users will find very easy to use.


LOCALIZATION

EFFICIENCY

The application will provide profiles of registered workers which will help users who are looking to hire workers in finding the best and suitable worker in an efficient manner.

How It Works

  • HOW CAN I FIND WORK?
    Posted by : iDreamz

    If you have a normal phone or a smart phone, all you have to do is access our application via the USSD code and fill in some basic details about you when prompted to. You will be asked to enter you name, what kind of work you're looking for, hourly wage expectation, district you live in, district you preffer to work in. After that you will be registered and a profile will be allocated to you with your basic details. After registering to the application you will receive messages when an employer wants to hire you. Until you accept the job and confirm it, all communication will be done through our application without both parties having their contact information given to each other. After we confirm that both parties have come to an arrangement we will give the employer the contact details of the worker and deduct a small fee from both parties. If workers want to find more jobs make sure you do your work well because your employers will be rating you, and the ratings will be displayed on your profiles, where new employers will be viewing frequently when they want to hire a worker. So make sure you get high ratings.

  • HOW CAN I FIND A WORKER?
    Posted by : iDreamz

    Access our application via the USSD code and select the required categories according to your needs and go through profiles of hundreds of workers. It's your choice whether you want to find workers living nearest to you so that they can reach you quickly if it's an urgent job, or workers with high ratings which ensures that they are very good at their work, or workers who charge a low amount of money for the work they are doing. So you get to select the worker of your choice. you. After selecting a worker, a message will be sent to them via the application and until they accept the job and confirm it, all communication will be done through our application without both parties having their contact information given to each other. After we confirm that both parties have come to an arrangement we will give the employer the contact details of the worker and deduct a small fee from both parties. After you get the work done, you have the ability to rate the worker, so that other emplyoyers know that the specific worker is good at what they are doing.
